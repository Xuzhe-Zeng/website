Acoustic Porosity Mapping in Laser Powder Bed Fusion

Developed a machine learning pipeline that maps airborne acoustic signals to sub-scanline porosity in LPBF using synchronized sensor and CT data.

  • My contribution: Designed full pipeline from signal preprocessing to CT-aligned porosity prediction; led training, evaluation, and ablation analysis.
  • Tech stack: Python, PyTorch, NumPy, Matplotlib, MATLAB
  • Outcome/Impact: Achieved sub-scanline porosity quantification; results accepted as arXiv preprint (2025).
